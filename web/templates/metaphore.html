Metaphore.

Metaphore iOS application offers you a simple and versatile way to model the space around using your own domain-specific description. Morover Metaphore is meant to be a customizable tool that enables the creation of domain-specific, mode-customized positioning-based modelling tools.

Metaphore is an iOS application for mobile devices, soon avalible on AppStore marketplace. Regarding its operation, Metaphore itself manages the privacy requirements of the operating system to use location services; the users, credentials, roles and access permissions to information; the work sessions; the available iBeacon devices; and the storage of models in memory.
