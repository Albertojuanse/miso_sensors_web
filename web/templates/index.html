Metaphore.An iOS app to semantize your world.

Metaphore iOS application offers you a simple and versatile way to model the space around using your own domain-specific description. It uses inertial sensors, compass, GPS and iBeacon devices to create fully customized positioning-based models.

Positioning-Based Modelling. A Model-driven engineering approach.

Modelling is a central activity in many disciplines, and some domain-specific modelling scenarios require a faithful representation of the position of the model elements in the physical world. To deal with these scenarios, Metaphore distinguish two dimensions for objects: a domain-specific dimension (the semantics provided by domain meta-models) and its physical facets (position calculated by the means of sensors and RF devices). This proccess is often called "semantize" the adquire data.
